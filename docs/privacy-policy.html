Privacy Policy

Noshift · Last updated: April 2026

Noshift ("we", "our", or "us") is a focus and commitment tracking app. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1 · Information we collect

We collect only what is necessary to run the app:

  • Account information — your email address when you sign up.
  • Focus data — the titles, deadlines, and outcomes of focuses you create.
  • Check-in data — whether you worked on your focus each day, and an optional momentum rating (1–5).
  • Parked ideas — ideas you choose to save for later.
  • Push notification token — a device token used to send you daily check-in reminders, if you enable notifications. We do not use this for any other purpose.

We do not collect location data, contacts, camera access, or any other device data.

2 · How we use your information

  • To provide the core app functionality (focus tracking, streaks, history).
  • To send you daily check-in reminder notifications, only when you have enabled them.
  • To show you aggregate usage stats in our admin dashboard (visible only to Noshift administrators).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

3 · Data storage and security

All data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS, US region). Access is protected by row-level security — you can only read and write your own data. Passwords are never stored in plain text; authentication is handled by Supabase Auth.

4 · Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us (see Section 7). We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

5 · Children's privacy

Noshift is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6 · Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
